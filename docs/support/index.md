# Support

Need help with DMFlow? We're here to assist you.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How do I add a new prospect?**
A: Tap the "+" button on the Today or Pipeline screen, enter your prospect's details, and tap Save.

**Q: Can I use DMFlow on multiple devices?**
A: Currently, DMFlow stores data locally on each device. Cloud sync is coming in a future update.

**Q: How do I back up my data?**
A: Go to Settings > Export Data to download a JSON file containing all your prospects.

### Subscriptions

**Q: What's included in DMFlow Pro?**
A: DMFlow Pro gives you unlimited AI-powered message suggestions to help craft the perfect follow-up messages.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
A: Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel.

### AI Features

**Q: How do AI message suggestions work?**
A: When you tap "Suggest Message" on a prospect, DMFlow uses AI to analyze their name, stage, and notes to generate personalized follow-up messages.

**Q: Is my data safe when using AI features?**
A: Yes. Your data is transmitted securely and is not stored on any servers. See our [Privacy Policy](/dmflow-ios/privacy/) for details.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app is running slowly. What should I do?**
A: Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, ensure you're running the latest version from the App Store.

**Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?**
A: Please email us at support@dmflow.app with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version.
